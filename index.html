The Team

The team consisted of 28 players in the 2021-2022 season. Although all showed value at some point in the season, the primary focus of the group is on the 'Core Four'

34

The talking about the Leafs team, the first player that comes to mind is Auston Matthews. He was the first overall pick in 2016, which was just the spark this team needed in the midst of a rebuild. Setting a franchise record of 60 goals in 73 games played in 2021-2022. Sporting number 34, Matthews is soilidified into the heart and soul of Leafs Nation.

16

To the suprise of nobody, Mitch Marner has become quite the star on the Toronto Maple Leafs. The Markham native paved his way to the NHL by dominating his way to a memorial cup with the OHL's London Knights. His high hockey IQ and slick moves makes it impossible to take your eyes off of him when hes on the ice.

91

A historic signing in the summer of 2018 brought the first captain of the Toronto Maples Leafs in years. Being the first overall pick in 2009 by the New York Islanders, John Tavares developed into an elite play-maker and leader on and off the ice.
